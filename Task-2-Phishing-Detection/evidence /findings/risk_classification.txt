Email Risk Classification

Email 1 – Account Verification
Risk Level: PHISHING
Reason: Multiple phishing indicators including suspicious domain, urgency tactics, and malicious link.

Email 2 – Payroll Update
Risk Level: PHISHING
Reason: Requests sensitive payroll data, uses fear tactics, and contains suspicious external link.

Email 3 – Microsoft 365 Password Expiry
Risk Level: PHISHING
Reason: Header authentication failures (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), impersonation of Microsoft, and malicious sender infrastructure.
